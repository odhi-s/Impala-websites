About IMPALA CONSTRUCTION COMPANY

Founded in 2013 as a small company by a group of 'fundis' with outstanding experience in the construction industry ,we started by building local residential houses and taking subcontract from other well established companies. We are thrilled to build you high-quality residential homes of modern design in kisumu and nyanza as a whole, including other parts of Kenya as well.Our main goal is to provide homes that blend :style ,functionality ,durability ,and integrity as a whole .Our commitment to quality, safety, and customer satisfaction sets us apart.

We specialize in both residential and commercial construction, with a team of experienced professionals dedicated to bringing your vision to life.
